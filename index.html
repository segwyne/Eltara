Welcome to Eltara

Eltara is a Live action role-playing game, where you can be anything that you're not.

We are a boffer-based, multi-genre game, where dimensions collide and post-apocalyptic meets pre-industrial meets high tech.

Come explore your imagination as you delve into other worlds.
