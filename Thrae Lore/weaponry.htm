Weaponry

            There are many different kinds of weapons.  Only weapons described here may be used in this game.  Most toy weapons will be allowed if they follow these basic guidelines:

  1. Missile weapons must have no hard spots anywhere.  These must be made of foam with no plastic, cardboard, or metal parts.
  2. Melee weapons must be covered in foam, with no protruding hard spots.

All weapons of any kind must be approved by a safety marshal before every game.  If you are unsure whether a weapon will be allowed or not, please ask a safety marshal.

Before every event, all weapons must be approved and tagged by a safety marshal.
