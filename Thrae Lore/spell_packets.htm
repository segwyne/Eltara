Spell Packets

Materials:

9" square piece of cloth
Parakeet seed
Rubber band

1.  Measure 1 tablespoon of parakeet seed into the center of the cloth.

2.  Gather up the edges around the seed and secure with rubber band.

Done.

In Eltara, the color of the packet indicates the type of magic or item it is.

Red Fire magic
Orange Alchemy
Yellow Air Magic
Green Earth Magic
Blue Water Magic
Purple Psionics
White Spirit Magic
Brown Arrows
